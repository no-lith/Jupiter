Effect Sample

This sample demostrates the use of the EffectShader system

Controls:
W or Up           : Move forward
A or Left         : Move left
S or Down         : Move back
D or Right        : Move right
1                 : Enable Black and White fullscreen shader
2                 : Enable Sepia tone fullscreen shader
3                 : Enable Sobel edge filter fullscreen shader
4                 : Enable Inverse color fullscreen shader
<shift> + move    : Move at twice the rate
<ctrl> + move     : Move at half the rate
