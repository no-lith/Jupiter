Drawprim Sample

This sample demostrates the use of the DrawPrim interface as a means for creating procedural terrain. 
The height map data in this sample was created from USGS data.

Controls:
W or Up           : Move forward
A or Left         : Move left
S or Down         : Move back
D or Right        : Move right
6                 : Switch to terrain
7                 : Switch to terrain
